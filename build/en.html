Highly technological units Kriger operate in automatic mode. Solid fuel is used in them: coal of different types, lump and brick peat, vegetable-based organic fuel (sunflower, buckwheat husk, corn wastes etc.), sawdust, wood pellets etc. They are characterized by high efficiency and option of remote control from the control station. Thermal capacity of a boiler unit is 1-8 MW.
These boilers provide production of heat with increased temperature to be used in technological processes. The coolant in this case is thermal oil, which is heated to 350С. The capacity of Kriger units is from 1 to 8 MW. Brown coal, sod brick peat, waste of vegetables processing, wood chips, sawdust, pellets etc. are used as fuel. The unit allows providing services for several consumers at a time, supplying heat of different temperature.
Fully automatic water-heating units Kriger, designed to provide for technological processes or heating systems, operate on several types of fuel with humidity to 55%. The coolant used is: coal, lump and brick peat, food production wastes (sunflower, buckwheat husk, corn cobs), wooden chips, sawdust, pellets etc. The unit can be controlled remotely via Internet.
Boiler units Kriger to produce saturated or overheated steam, required for technological processes and maintenance of heating systems and hot water supply. The capacity of the equipment is from 1.6 to 10 tons of steam per hour. The coolant used is: brown and black coal, lump and brick peat, sunflower husk, corn wastes, sawdust, pellets etc. The boilers are equipped with automatic fuel supply and ash removal system.
For the adaptationof the existing natural gas, coal and fuel oil equipment to alternative solid biofuels the furnace extensions with moving grate are used. The design allows you to completely burn the fuel with humidity of15-55%with a high heat release. The efficiency is achieved by the special configuration of the combustion chamber, made of reinforce concrete with alumina. The automated system controls the fuel distributionand removal of ash and smoke.
For the organization of the smooth operation of the boiler room for four daysthe stockyards areused. Their dimensions is calculateddepending on fuel volatility. They may be located in a separate room or in a metal silo. The automatic continuous solid biofuelsupply is provided by the automated fuel supply system "Live bottom" with a scraper conveyor, a plunger pump, a spiral conveyor, a flanged pan feeder, the automated fuel supply system "Silo" and the “Leaf-spring” automated fuel supply system.

Boiler-house "Denyshy"

The main equipment of the project is water heating boiler Kriger using dry biological fuel.

Coolant Hot water
Supply temperature (operational) 115С
Return temperature 30С
Coolant working pressure 0,6 МПа
Capacity at full load 1 500 кВт
Annual fuel consumption 7 680 м3
Acceptable fuel humidity 55%

The main types of fuel are wood chips and milled peat. Metal particles, stones and sand are not allowed. The system is equipped with automatic cleaning of the boiler from dirt accumulation and combustion control. The warehouse with automatic fuel supply is designated for uninterrupted operation for four days. The complex completely provides the medical facility with hot water and heating for household and technological needs.
